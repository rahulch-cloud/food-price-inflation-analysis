KEY FINDINGS:

1. WORST DECADE EVER: 2020s
   • Average Inflation: 8.62% (highest in 50 years!)
   • Volatility: 23.98% (double the 1970s!)
   • This is WORSE than the 1970s oil crisis

2. BEST DECADE: 1990s
   • Average: -0.17% (DEFLATION!)
   • Farmers/producers lost money during globalization

3. EXTREME EVENT:
   • Biggest Spike: 163.1%
   • Category: Farm-level eggs
   • Year: 2022

4. MOST VOLATILE FOOD:
   • Farm-level eggs: 31.17% volatility

5. PRODUCER CRISIS:
   • 2020s producer prices are WORSE than consumer prices
   • Farmers absorbing losses that aren't passed to consumers
   • Expect future retail price increases
